Hungarian NDA Template — Titoktartási Megállapodás

📚 Legal basis: Act V of 2013 (Civil Code / Ptk.) §§ 6:77–6:81; Act LIV of 2018 on Trade Secrets (Üzleti Titok Törvény); EU Trade Secrets Directive 2016/943

English Version — Non-Disclosure Agreement

NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (TITOKTARTÁSI MEGÁLLAPODÁS) This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] by and between: [PARTY A — DISCLOSING PARTY] Full legal name: ___________________________________ Registered address: ________________________________ Company registration number (cégjegyzékszám): _________ Represented by: ___________________________________ (hereinafter: "Disclosing Party") AND [PARTY B — RECEIVING PARTY] Full legal name: ___________________________________ Registered address: ________________________________ Company registration number (cégjegyzékszám): _________ Represented by: ___________________________________ (hereinafter: "Receiving Party") (Each a "Party" and together the "Parties") 1. PURPOSE The Parties intend to [describe the purpose, e.g. "evaluate a potential commercial collaboration / technology licensing arrangement"] (the "Purpose"). In connection with the Purpose, the Disclosing Party may disclose Confidential Information to the Receiving Party. 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION "Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, that: (a) is marked as "Confidential", "Proprietary" or similar; or (b) is disclosed orally and identified as confidential at the time of disclosure; or (c) would reasonably be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure, including but not limited to: business plans, financial data, customer lists, technical specifications, trade secrets, know-how, software, and pricing information. Confidential Information does NOT include information that: (i) is or becomes publicly available without breach of this Agreement; (ii) was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records; (iii) is lawfully received from a third party without restriction; (iv) is required to be disclosed by applicable law, court order, or regulatory authority — provided the Receiving Party gives prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party where legally permitted. 3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE RECEIVING PARTY The Receiving Party agrees to: (a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential using at least the same degree of care as it uses for its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care; (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party; (c) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose; (d) Disclose Confidential Information only to its employees, directors, advisers, or contractors who have a need to know for the Purpose and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement; (e) Notify the Disclosing Party promptly upon becoming aware of any unauthorised disclosure or use of Confidential Information. 4. TERM This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and continue for [3] years, unless terminated earlier by either Party on [30] days' written notice. Obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination for a period of [5] years. 5. RETURN OF MATERIALS Upon the Disclosing Party's written request, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information (in all forms) and certify such destruction in writing. 6. NO LICENCE Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any licence, right, title or interest in the Confidential Information or any intellectual property of the Disclosing Party. 7. REMEDIES AND LIABILITY The Parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages may be inadequate. The Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief (előzetes intézkedés iránti kérelem) in addition to other remedies available under Hungarian law. Liability for breach shall be governed by Act V of 2013 (Ptk.) §§ 6:142–6:152. 8. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Hungary. Any dispute arising in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Hungarian courts [or: submitted to arbitration before the Permanent Court of Arbitration attached to the Hungarian Chamber of Commerce and Industry (MKIK) under its Rules]. 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties. 10. SEVERABILITY If any provision of this Agreement is found invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force. Signed by the Parties: DISCLOSING PARTY RECEIVING PARTY Name: ___________________ Name: ___________________ Title: ___________________ Title: ___________________ Date: ___________________ Date: ___________________ Signature: _______________ Signature: _______________

IDŐTARTAM E Megállapodás a fent megjelölt napon lép hatályba és [3] évig marad érvényben, kivéve, ha bármelyik Fél [30] napos előzetes írásbeli felmondással korábban megszünteti. A titoktartási kötelezettség a megszűnést követő [5] évig hatályban marad. 5. ANYAGOK VISSZASZOLGÁLTATÁSA Az Átadó Fél írásbeli kérésére az Átvevő Fél haladéktalanul visszaszolgáltatja vagy megsemmisíti a Bizalmas Információ minden hordozóját (minden formában) és a megsemmisítést írásban igazolja. 6. SZELLEMI TULAJDON E Megállapodás az Átvevő Félnek semmilyen licence jogot, tulajdonjogot vagy más jogot nem biztosít a Bizalmas Információval vagy az Átadó Fél szellemi tulajdonával kapcsolatban. 7. JOGORVOSLAT ÉS FELELŐSSÉG A Felek elismerik, hogy e Megállapodás megsértése olyan jóvátehetetlen kárt okozhat, amelyre a pénzbeli kártérítés nem nyújt megfelelő orvoslatot. Az Átadó Fél a pénzbeli kártérítés mellett előzetes intézkedés iránti kérelmet is benyújthat. A felelősséget a Polgári Törvénykönyvről szóló 2013. évi V. törvény (Ptk.) 6:142–6:152. §-ai szabályozzák. 8. ALKALMAZANDÓ JOG ÉS ILLETÉKESSÉG E Megállapodásra a magyar jog az irányadó. Frequently Asked Questions — Hungarian NDAs

Is an NDA legally enforceable in Hungary?

Yes. NDAs (titoktartási megállapodás) are governed by the Hungarian Civil Code (Act V of 2013 — Ptk.), specifically §§ 6:77–6:81 on confidentiality obligations. The Act LIV of 2018 on Trade Secrets (Üzleti Titok Törvény) implements EU Directive 2016/943 and provides additional protection for qualifying trade secrets. Hungarian courts routinely enforce NDAs; breach gives rise to damages under Ptk. §§ 6:142–6:152.

Does a Hungarian NDA need to be notarised?

No — standard NDAs in Hungary do not require notarisation or an ügyvédi countersignature. They are enforceable as ordinary written contracts under the Ptk. Electronic signatures are valid under Act CCXXII of 2015 on Electronic Administration (e-ügyintézési törvény). For enforcement in foreign courts, a certified translation and apostille under the 1961 Hague Convention may be required.

What is the penalty for breaching an NDA in Hungary?

Under Ptk. §§ 6:142–6:152, the innocent party may claim: (1) actual damages (tényleges kár + lost profits elmaradt haszon); (2) the breaching party's unjust enrichment; or (3) a contractual penalty (kötbér) if specified. Hungarian courts apply the full-compensation principle (teljes kártalanítás elve). An injunction (előzetes intézkedés) is available under Act CXXX of 2016 on Civil Procedure (Pp.) § 156.

Retainer Letter — Megbízási Levél (Skeleton)

📚 Legal basis: Act LXXXIV of 2007 on Lawyers (Ügyvédi Törvény); Act V of 2013 (Ptk.) §§ 6:272–6:280 on Mandate (Megbízás)

Use this skeleton to establish the scope, fees, and terms of an engagement with a Hungarian ügyvéd before formal instructions begin.

Retainer / Engagement Letter (English)

FEES Our fees for this matter will be calculated as follows: [Select one or more:] ☐ Fixed fee: HUF _______________ (plus VAT/ÁFA at 27% if applicable) ☐ Hourly rate: HUF _______ per hour for partner; HUF _______ per hour for associate ☐ Percentage of transaction value: ______% of the purchase price ☐ Monthly retainer: HUF _______________ per month for [SCOPE] Disbursements (land registry fees, stamp duties, translation, courier costs) will be charged at cost. 3. ⚖ What to check in any Hungarian retainer letter

  • Bar registration number (kamarai szám): Verify the ügyvéd is registered at magyarugyvedikamara.hu — online search is free
  • Scope is specific: Vague retainers lead to disputes. Ensure exactly what is included and excluded is written out
  • VAT (ÁFA) treatment: Confirm whether fees are quoted inclusive or exclusive of 27% ÁFA — significant for business clients
  • KYC/AML compliance: Under the 2017 Money Laundering Act (Pmt.), lawyers must verify your identity. This is mandatory, not optional
  • Disbursements cap: Consider requesting a cap on disbursements (translation, couriers) to avoid surprises
  • Termination rights: Under Ptk. §§ 6:272–6:280, mandates (megbízás) may be terminated by either party — confirm the notice period

Complaint Letter to Hungarian Bar Association

📚 Legal basis: Act LXXXIV of 2007 on Lawyers (Ügyvédi Törvény) §§ 115–130; Hungarian Bar Association Disciplinary Code (Fegyelmi Szabályzat)

If your Hungarian lawyer has acted improperly — overcharged, failed to communicate, breached confidentiality, or acted with a conflict of interest — you may file a formal complaint with the relevant regional bar association.

Formal Complaint Letter (English — to be translated into Hungarian)

FORMAL COMPLAINT AGAINST AN ÜGYVÉD (FEGYELMI FELJELENTÉS / PANASZ) [YOUR NAME] [YOUR ADDRESS] [YOUR EMAIL / PHONE] [DATE] To: The Chairman / Disciplinary Committee [Regional Bar Association — e.g. Budapesti Ügyvédi Kamara] [ADDRESS] RE: Formal Complaint against [LAWYER'S FULL NAME], ügyvéd Bar registration number: [KAMARAI SZÁM] Law firm: [FIRM NAME AND ADDRESS] Dear Sir / Madam, I write to submit a formal complaint against the above-named ügyvéd pursuant to Act LXXXIV of 2007 on Lawyers (Ügyvédi Törvény) and the Hungarian Bar Association's Ethical Code (Etikai Szabályzat). 1. RELATIONSHIP WITH THE RESPONDENT I engaged [LAWYER'S NAME] of [FIRM NAME] on [DATE] to assist with [brief description of the legal matter, e.g. "the purchase of a residential property at [ADDRESS]"]. A copy of the engagement letter / retainer agreement is attached as Exhibit 1. 2. NATURE OF THE COMPLAINT I make the following complaints against [LAWYER'S NAME]: [COMPLAINT A — EXAMPLE: OVERCHARGING] The lawyer charged a total of HUF [AMOUNT], which I believe was not in accordance with the agreed fee of HUF [AGREED AMOUNT] / [PERCENTAGE]% as set out in the engagement letter. Despite written requests on [DATE] and [DATE], I have not received an itemised fee breakdown. Copies of the invoices and my correspondence are attached as Exhibits 2 and 3. [COMPLAINT B — EXAMPLE: FAILURE TO COMMUNICATE] Between [START DATE] and [END DATE], I sent [NUMBER] emails and made [NUMBER] telephone calls to the lawyer's office without receiving a substantive response. As a result, [describe the consequence, e.g. "I missed the deadline to submit documents to the land registry"]. Evidence of unanswered communications is attached as Exhibit 4. [COMPLAINT C — EXAMPLE: CONFLICT OF INTEREST] I subsequently discovered that [LAWYER'S NAME] was simultaneously acting for [OTHER PARTY] in the same transaction / a related matter, without disclosing this conflict to me. This violates §[X] of the Etikai Szabályzat and §[X] of the Ügyvédi Törvény. [COMPLAINT D — EXAMPLE: BREACH OF CONFIDENTIALITY] The lawyer disclosed confidential information relating to my matter to [THIRD PARTY] without my consent, in breach of §9 of the Ügyvédi Törvény (ügyvédi titoktartás). 3. REQUESTED REMEDY I respectfully request that the Bar Association: (a) Investigate this complaint and initiate disciplinary proceedings where appropriate; (b) Order the lawyer to provide an itemised fee breakdown [if applicable]; (c) [Any specific remedy sought]; (d) Inform me of the outcome of the investigation. 4. SUPPORTING DOCUMENTS (MELLÉKLETEK) Exhibit 1: Engagement letter / retainer agreement Exhibit 2: Invoices received Exhibit 3: My written correspondence with the lawyer Exhibit 4: [Other evidence] 5. DECLARATION I confirm that the information in this complaint is true and accurate to the best of my knowledge. I understand that knowingly providing false information in disciplinary proceedings may itself constitute a breach of applicable rules. Yours faithfully, [YOUR SIGNATURE] [YOUR NAME (PRINT)] [DATE] --- IMPORTANT NOTES: • This letter should be translated into Hungarian (certified translation recommended) before submission. • The relevant Bar Association depends on where the ügyvéd is registered: - Budapest: Budapesti Ügyvédi Kamara, 1054 Budapest, Szemere utca 8, Tel: +36 1 353 0240 - Pest County: Pest Megyei Ügyvédi Kamara - Győr: Győri Ügyvédi Kamara - (For other regions: see magyarugyvedikamara.hu) • Time limit: Disciplinary complaints must generally be filed within 3 years of the breach. • Alternative: For fee disputes specifically, consider applying to the Bar Association's fee arbitration service (díjvita) which is quicker than formal disciplinary proceedings. • NAIH complaint: If your complaint relates to GDPR/data protection, file separately with the National Authority for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (Nemzeti Adatvédelmi és Információszabadság Hatóság — NAIH) at naih.hu.
Before filing a complaint:
  1. First raise the issue directly with the lawyer or the firm's supervising partner — most disputes can be resolved at this stage
  2. Keep all written records: emails, letters, invoices, and notes of phone calls with dates and times
  3. For fee disputes, request an itemised bill (tételes számla) before escalating
  4. The Bar's díjvita (fee arbitration) service handles overcharging complaints faster than disciplinary proceedings
  5. Formal disciplinary proceedings can take 6–18 months; set realistic expectations
