Kecskemét Legal & Property Market Overview

The capital of Bács-Kiskun county and a major automotive and agricultural hub of the Great Plain. Kecskemét has seen Győr-like industrial investment with the Mercedes-Benz factory driving expat demand. Agricultural land in the surrounding Bács-Kiskun region is subject to Hungary's strict agricultural land protection laws — non-EU citizens cannot purchase agricultural land.

Frequently Asked Questions — Kecskemét

Can foreigners buy agricultural land near Kecskemét?
EU nationals with agricultural qualifications or those who have farmed in Hungary for at least 3 years may purchase agricultural land subject to Land Commission approval. Non-EU nationals are prohibited from purchasing agricultural land (szántó, rét, legelő, erdő) under Act CCXII of 2013. Both groups CAN buy residential property (lakóingatlan) without restriction — it is the land use classification that matters, not location.
What is a tanya and can foreigners buy one?
A tanya is a traditional Great Plain farmstead — often a house with several hectares of attached agricultural land. The residential building component can be purchased by foreigners but the agricultural land portion requires Chamber approval and residency commitments. Your lawyer must structure the purchase to separate the residential and agricultural portions under Act CXXII of 2013.
How does Mercedes-Benz affect Kecskemét property?
Mercedes-Benz Hungária employs 5,000+ with a large number of German and international engineers. This drives demand for quality furnished rentals. The factory's recent EV transition investment (announced 2024) is expected to bring further international staff. 2-bed furnished apartments near the plant achieve gross yields of 5–7%.
