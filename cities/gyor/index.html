Győr Legal & Property Market Overview

Western Hungary's industrial powerhouse and one of the fastest-growing cities in Central Europe. Győr benefits from proximity to Vienna and the Audi factory (50,000+ employees in the region). Property demand is strong from German and Austrian buyers and Audi expatriates. Prices are rising faster than most Hungarian cities.

Frequently Asked Questions — Győr

Why is Győr property in high demand among Austrian buyers?
Győr is just 80 km from Vienna — within commuting distance for some workers — but property prices are 60–70% lower than Vienna. Hungarian properties can be purchased by Austrian citizens (EU nationals) without any permit. The only requirement is lawyer countersignature (ellenjegyzés) and land registry filing. Many Austrians buy second homes or investment properties here.
What border zone rules apply to Győr property purchases?
Non-EU nationals face a 10 km restriction zone near Hungary's borders with Austria and Slovakia (both Schengen external borders). Győr city centre is outside this zone but check with your lawyer for specific addresses. OIF permit applications for border zone properties take 30–60 days under Government Decree 251/2014.
How does the Audi factory affect Győr's property market?
Audi Hungária employs over 11,000 directly and tens of thousands in its supplier network. The company regularly brings in European engineers on multi-year assignments, creating strong expat rental demand. Furnished 2-bed apartments near the factory (Győr-Gyárváros quarter) achieve gross yields of 6–8%.
