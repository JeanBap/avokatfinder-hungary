About AvökatFinder Hungary

An independent English-language directory of verified law firms in Hungary for expats and foreign nationals.

What is AvökatFinder?

AvökatFinder is an independent online directory designed to help expats, foreign nationals and international businesses find the right English-speaking law firm in Hungary. We do not accept payment for placement — all rankings are based on Chambers Europe, the Legal 500 EMEA and IFLR1000 data.

How We Verify Firms

Every firm listed in this directory has been verified against at least one of the following: Chambers Europe rankings, Legal 500 EMEA rankings, IFLR1000 Financial Law rankings, the US Embassy Budapest recommended lawyer list, or bar association registration. We prioritise firms that explicitly offer English-language services.

Disclaimer

AvökatFinder is a directory service, not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice. The information on this site is for informational purposes only. Always verify a firm's details directly before instructing them. Law firm rankings and contact details may change — we strive to keep information current but cannot guarantee accuracy.

Contact

For corrections, additions or queries, contact us at info@avokatfinder.com.
